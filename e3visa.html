What is the E-3 Visa?

The E-3 Visa is a special work visa available exclusively to Australian citizens, offering a streamlined path to employment in the US. Check the official USCIS website for more information.

Comparison: E-3 Visa vs. H-1B Visa

The E-3 visa has many advantages over the H-1B visa.

Aspect E-3 Visa H-1B Visa
Eligibility Exclusive to Australian citizens Open to all nationalities
Annual Quota 10,500 new visas (less than 50% used each year) 85,000 visas (always oversubscribed)
Processing Time 4 to 6 weeks (faster) Lengthy process, involving a lottery
Validity Renewable every 2 years, indefinitely Capped at 6 years
Cost Visa fee is $315 Can cost thousands
Spouse Work Authorization Yes, spouses can work No, spouses need separate approval
